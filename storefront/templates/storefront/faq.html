Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, M-Pesa mobile payments, and cash on delivery.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and work with trusted payment processors. We never store your credit card information on our servers.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days within Kenya. Express shipping is available for faster delivery.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within Kenya. International shipping may be available in the future.

What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs vary based on location and order size. Standard shipping is KES 200, express shipping is KES 500.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

You can return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided they are unused and in original packaging.

How do I return an item?

Contact our customer service team to initiate a return. We'll provide a return label and instructions.

How long do refunds take?

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Size & Fit

How do I find my shoe size?

Check our size guide for detailed measurements. We recommend measuring your feet for the most accurate fit.

What if my shoes don't fit?

You can exchange for a different size within our return window, subject to availability.

Do you offer half sizes?

Yes, we carry half sizes for most of our shoes. Check the product page for available sizes.

Account & Orders

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation and fill out the registration form with your details.

How do I track my order?

Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to see tracking information for your purchases.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can only be modified within 1 hour of placement. Contact customer service immediately if needed.

Product Information

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized manufacturers and distributors.

Do you offer warranties?

Most of our shoes come with manufacturer warranties. Check the product description for specific warranty information.

Can I customize shoes?

Currently, we offer standard sizes and colors. Custom options may be available for select items.
